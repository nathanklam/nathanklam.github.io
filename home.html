A combat robot with a 4-wheel drive, a dual-disk spinner weapon, and angled armor to deflect impacts. Won "Coolest Bot" in 2025 at Sacramento Bot Battles.

1lb Combat Robots: “Skadoosh” and “Eggsterminator”

Eggsterminator is a 1lb plastic combat robot with a 2 wheel drive and angled armor; won "Most Destructive" in an internal competition. Skadoosh is the second iteration, utilizing an improved weapon while experimenting with unconventional geometry for the front wedge.

Tools/Skills: Fusion 360 · 3D Printing · Soldering · GD&T
