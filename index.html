About Me

I am a second-year undergard student majoring in Mechanical Engineering and minoring in EECS. I'm interested in mechanical design, manufacturing, prototyping, and how those aspects connect to electronics. I enjoy exploring new and sometimes unconventional ideas and turning them into physical parts through CAD, 3D printing, and iterative testing.

A research project in progress, investigating the behavior of composites under medium-velocity impacts (up to 110 m/s) with non-linear trajectories and high mass. The goal is to explore and possibly develop alternatives to aluminum 6061 and steel for armor applications in combat robotics.
