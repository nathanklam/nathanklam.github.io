15lb Combat Robot: “Master Oogway”

Overview

Master Oogway is a 15 lb combat robot, featuring a belt-driven dual-disk AR500 steel weapon, 4-wheel drive, and angled armor optimized for deflection and survivability. The design prioritizes modularity, manufacturability, and rapid repair between matches.

Design Process

As part of the 2nd semester at Combat Robotics at Berkeley, members such as I applied and were accepted to form new teams to design a 15 lb robot, which greatly expanded the possibilities for what could be designed compared to plastic 1lb robots.

Due to our limited budget of $1000 dollars and the capabilities student organization's manufacturing sponsors, we were essentially constrained to designing for 2D manufacturing.
